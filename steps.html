STEP 1

Gather Ingredients: You'll need fresh basil leaves, pine nuts, garlic, Parmesan cheese (or Pecorino Romano), extra virgin olive oil, salt, pepper, and your choice of pasta.

STEP 2

Prepare Ingredients: Wash and dry the basil leaves. Grate the Parmesan cheese. Toast the pine nuts in a dry skillet over medium heat until they are lightly golden and fragrant. Peel and crush the garlic cloves

STEP 3

Make Pesto Sauce: In a food processor or blender, combine the basil leaves, toasted pine nuts, crushed garlic, and grated Parmesan cheese. Pulse until the ingredients are finely chopped and combined.

STEP 4

Add Olive Oil: With the food processor running, gradually drizzle in the extra virgin olive oil until the mixture forms a smooth paste. You may need to stop and scrape down the sides of the bowl as needed. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

STEP 5

Cook Pasta: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add your pasta and cook according to package instructions until al dente.

STEP 6

Combine: Once the pasta is cooked, drain it well, reserving some of the pasta cooking water. Transfer the drained pasta back to the pot.

STEP 7

Mix in Pesto: Add the pesto sauce to the cooked pasta and toss until the pasta is evenly coated. If the sauce is too thick, you can add a splash of the reserved pasta cooking water to thin it out and help it adhere to the pasta

STEP 8

Serve: Divide the pesto pasta among serving plates or bowls. You can garnish with additional grated Parmesan cheese, a drizzle of olive oil, and some fresh basil leaves if desired.

FINISH

Enjoy: Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious homemade pesto pasta!
